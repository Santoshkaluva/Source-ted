Speakers


The Curation Team is responsible for the Speakers co - ordination and also making the speaker be aware of the Theme. We have organized every TEDx event which has been staged at the Campus followed by a theme. We believe that organizing the Event, by keeping the Theme in mind makes the Talk more promising as well as the Event.For TEDxGITAMUniversity 2015 the theme is Inside Out !

Here are the list of Speakers for the year 2014 along with their profiles.




Here are the list of Speakers for the year 2013 along with their profiles.


Sapna Vyas Patel

Sapna Vyas Patel is a doctoral student and a Reebok Certified Fitness Professional. Using her experience and knowledge, so far she has helped over 5000 people through Facebook in reaching their fitness goal without charging a single penny.Her's is an inspiring story, having lost 33 KG in an year .

Binalakshmi Nepram

Binalakshmi Nepramis a writer-activist and is an emerging determined face and strong voice in the region who is spearheading work on making disarmament a movement and an issue that is meaningful to people's lives. Ms Nepram has published several articles and papers in both national and international journals on issues relating to armed violence, small arms proliferation, peace processes, women and peace building.

Karthick Iyer

Karthick Iyer is a classically trained vocalist-violinist-composer ,with an unusually strong command over music and technique. With over 20 years of training and practice, and a wide exposure to really diverse world music, this hardly comes as a surprise. Karthick has fascinated audiences in over a thousand concerts across ten countries with his lightning fast fingers and a deep, soulful voice - establishing himself as a force that is fundamentally Indian, but universal in approach and appeal.

Ram Mohan Adibhatla

A Teacher. A Friend. A Polymath. A Connoisseur of knowledge and learning. Ram Mohan Adibhatla started as an English Teacher. With many challenges he took in life, he succeeded in facing the odds. He worked hard, harder, even more harder in facing them, which he depicts the situation a "Rolling Stone". He now brings us what can be the Rolling Stones of success.

Sanjay Govilkar

Sanjay Govilkar is an assistant police inspector who along with other policemen captured Mohammed Ajmal Amir Iman and killed Ismail Khan, the terrorists involved in the 26\11 Mumbai attacks.In his own words , "We pounced on Kasab, Omble fell to bullets as Kasab fired and I too received a bullet injury. While I continued to hold Kasab's rifle with a strong grip, Omble gave up because he was seriously injured."

Dr Gurava Reddy

He is the Managing Director of Sunshine Hospitals, Secunderabad and Chief Joint Replacement Surgeon at Sunshine Bone and Joint Institute, a 300 bed NABH Accredited, Multispeciality hospital.He is the leading Hip and Knee Arthroplasty surgeon in India and performs about 3500 joint replacements per year (#2 largest numbers in Asia and more than any surgeon in Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore and Calcutta).He has made sustained efforts to increase the awareness and acceptance of Joint replacement surgery in India bringing relief to thousands of patients who were compromising on their quality of life due to pain and immobility from arthritic hips and knee.

Anand Gandhi

Anand Gandhi is an independent filmmaker and screenwriter based in Mumbai most known for his debut feature film Ship of Theseus which won the National Film Award for Best Picture, which is considered India's equivalent of the Best Picture Oscar. He was initially involved in parallel theatre, where he wrote and directed several critically acclaimed plays.

Ashwin Mohan

Ashwin Mohan is a chain entrepreneur.His expertise is in eliminating all forms of stress and violence. He has been training in martial arts for 32 years and holds 6 back belts and teacher’s certifications in over 24 other martial arts.He is the only teacher in the world to have taught over 5.5 million students self defence. This is largely due to his capacity as an entrepreneur.

Deepak Deen Dayal

Mr.Deepak Deen Dayal killed his landlord and was sentenced to jail. Later, after his jail term, he had the choice of becoming a gangster or a good person. Mr.Deepak chose to become a teacher.An ex-convict turned teacher , he is an inspiration for a lot of people.

Robin Chaurasiya

Robin is fighting to give a better life to the children of sex workers and similar kids from red light areas who have been affected by domestic violence.After leaving military three years ago, Robin came to India to start Kranti, The motivation for Kranti came from her last visit to the country when she was volunteering and saw the situation at grassroots level.

Sandeep Bachche

An auto driver from Bombay who revolutionized the concept of auto driving with his facilities and schemes.With an auto comprising a myraid of facilities ranging from a cell phone charger to a newspaper to movies on the LCD screen , he has it all. He also offers discounts to the elderly , apart from special offers on occassions like Valentine's day .

STM Veerbahu

STM Veerabahu has built a robotic limb which will assist individual’s suffering from Polio attack, amputated legs, or individuals suffering from weakened muscles in legs, spinal cord injured to “Walk”.The innovative work of the 33-year-old now figures in the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) Technology Review India TR35 list, in the computing category..

Prasanna Kumar

A.Prasanna Kumar, Director, Centre for Policy Studies is former Regional Director National Maritime Foundation, Visakhapatnam Chapter (September 2009 - September 2012), former President of Visakha Music Academy (June 2004 - June 2011), former President of Visakhapatnam Veterans Tennis Association (2000-2003) and former Rector and retired Professor of Politics, Andhra University. He was a member of the Academic Council of Sri Sathya Sai University, Prasanthi Nilayam for six years. He was All India Radio’s sports commentator from 1964-2000 on tennis, cricket and badminton. Among the running commentaries done by him were: ASIAD 82, First and Second Masters and Grand Prix in badminton, over ten Davis Cup ties and ATP Challenger tennis tournaments, and several cricket commentaries on zonal, national and international matches.

Anupama Hoskere

The founder of Dhaatu which works to instill in children eternal human values and relevant wisdoms through the medium of storytelling and personal interaction – utilizing the ample material for art and expression from India’s ancient wealth, captivating stories told through epics, upanishadic episodes and historical/mythological accounts of varied, colorful and inspiring personages. Anupama Hoskere will perform a puppet show and speak on how Puppetry Theater can promote Ancient Mythology. Discover the long forgotten art of storytelling with only voices and puppets.

Harish Iyer

A victim of child sexual abuse, survivor, gay rights activist, animal rights activist and an environmentalist. He is now a household name in every Indian home after his appearance in Amir Khan’s famous show Satyameva Jayate. Mr.Iyer may look like a shy person with dimpled cheeks but his courage is commendable. He will be with us to share his story and how he survived the trauma.

Ojas Suniti Vinay

A young theatre artist from Pune, Ms.Ojas has been spreading awareness about the plight of the dwellers of North East India. With excellent facial expressions and story-telling skills, Ms.Ojas has taken her one-act play ‘Le Mashale’ across the length and breadth of India, telling people the tale of Irom Sharmila, the famous Manipuri activist. She has been running a campaign against the Armed Forces Special Powers Act (AFSPA) even as a debate rages in Jammu & Kashmir and the Northeast over its implementation and rights violations.

Joseph Radhik

Joseph ‘Joe’ Radhik: One of India’s topmost wedding photographer. An Engineer and IIM grad, Joe is the best example of India’s creative youth. He has covered many celebrity weddings across the country, and he will be speaking about his journey in the Photography field.

Mitchell London

President and CEO at Headroom Learning, a leading educational software company, Mr.London has more than twenty years of experience managing high-growth technology ventures. After earning a BS degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Carnegie Mellon University in 1987, he began his career with Microsoft, where he played a key role in the early development of the Windows Operating System. In 1988, Mr.London founded ConnectSoft Inc., a multi-divisional firm located in Bellevue, Washington. He negotiated several financings and multi- million dollar business transactions. In 1996, Mr.London sold ConnectSoft to a NASDAQ listed technology holding company in a transaction valued at more than twenty million dollars.

Rakesh Reddy Dubbudu

A person with high social commitment, Rakesh holds a B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering from NIT, Warangal. He is the Founding Partner of BHUMI Hyderabad and is responsible for Project Planning and Execution in the field of Governance and RTI. He is the Andhra Pradesh Coordinator for the “National People's RTI Assessment 2008”. He was the winner of the First ever “Citizen Journalist Award” awarded by the Network 18 group.
